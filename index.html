Why Track Blood

Your health data, finally useful

Blood reports pile up in folders. Track Blood turns them into a single, clear timeline.

📈

See how you're trending

Upload reports from different dates and instantly see how your Hemoglobin, TSH, Vitamin D, or any marker has been moving — month over month, year over year.

🩺

Walk into your doctor prepared

Hand your doctor a single-page CSV with all parameters across time. They can spot a trend in seconds that would otherwise take 20 minutes of flipping through individual reports.

🤖

Get AI-powered insights

Drop the CSV into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini. Ask "what trends do you see?" or "which markers need attention?" Get a second opinion on your health trajectory instantly.

📂

Works across lab formats

Thyrocare, SRL, Metropolis, Apollo, Aarthi Scans — the parser recognises over 150 markers and their aliases, regardless of how each lab names or formats the test.

🔒 Your health data never leaves your device

Track Blood runs entirely inside your browser. There is no server, no account, no upload. Every PDF you drop stays on your machine — the app reads it locally, extracts the numbers, and hands you a CSV. Nothing is transmitted anywhere, ever.

Want to verify? Turn off your Wi-Fi and try it — it still works perfectly.
